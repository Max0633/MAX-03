什么是CPO？ What is CPO?

共封装光学（Co-Packaged Optics，CPO）是一种将光学引擎直接集成到交换芯片封装内的技术。相比传统可插拔光模块，CPO可降低30-50%功耗，显著提高带宽密度，是下一代数据中心光互联的关键方向。 Co-Packaged Optics (CPO) is a technology that integrates optical engines directly into switch chip packages. Compared to traditional pluggable optical modules, CPO can reduce power consumption by 30-50% and significantly increase bandwidth density, making it a key direction for next-generation data center optical interconnects.

核心优势 Key Advantages

  • 功耗降低30-50%Power consumption reduced by 30-50%
  • 带宽密度显著提高Significantly increased bandwidth density
  • 延迟降低Reduced latency
  • 信号完整性改善Improved signal integrity
